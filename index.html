Where Tradition Meets Innovation

Chef Joseph’s culinary journey began with a love for both Japanese precision and Mexican soul. The result? A distinctive Mexican-Hibachi fusion that brings the best of both worlds together—a dining style that’s lively, flavorful, and deeply personal.

At Joey’s Way, every meal is a performance of connection—between chef and guest, flavor and artistry. Chef Joseph believes that food isn’t just meant to be eaten—it’s meant to be experienced.
